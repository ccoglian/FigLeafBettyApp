    Kale Salad

    Results Make It!

    Kale Salad is my favorite dish! It's good for any meal or even for dessert. I make it about twelve thousand different ways so use your imagination and creativity to make it the way you like best!


    Ingredients

    1 Bunch Lacinato Kale or you can use any other type of kale you like
    2 Tablespoons EVOO or you can use half an avocado
    2 Teaspoons Sea Salt

    Directions

    1. Massage the kale as hard as you can until it gets darker and softer
    2. Put whatever you want on it like grapes or pepitas. Enjoy!
    Serves 4
